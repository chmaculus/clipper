'------------------------------------------------------------------------------
^BMIN()^B

    MIN() returns the smaller value of two numeric or date expressions.

^USyntax^U

    MIN(<exp N1>,<exp N2>)

^UUsage^U

    Both arguments must be of the same data type.

    The MIN() function compares two numeric expressions and returns the
    smaller of the two.
!seealso: clip_107.ngo:"MAX" 
'------------------------------------------------------------------------------
