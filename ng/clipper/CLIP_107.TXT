'------------------------------------------------------------------------------
^BMAX()^B

    MAX() returns the larger value of two numeric or date expressions.

^USyntax^U

    MAX(<exp N1>,<exp N2>)

^UUsage^U

    Both arguments must be of the same type.

    The MAX() function compares two numeric expressions and returns the
    largest of the two.

'------------------------------------------------------------------------------
