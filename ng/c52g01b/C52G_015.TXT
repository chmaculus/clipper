 ^b Notes^b

 ^B^CFE  Reserved words^b cannot be used for variable, procedure, or
    user-defined function names.

 ^B^CFE  Reserved functions^b are built into the compiler and therefore cannot
    be redefined by an application.

 ^B^CFE  Abbreviations^b of reserved words and functions of four or more
    characters are also reserved.

 ^B^CFE^b  All identifiers that begin with one or more underscore characters
    (_) are designated as system identifiers and are therefore reserved.
